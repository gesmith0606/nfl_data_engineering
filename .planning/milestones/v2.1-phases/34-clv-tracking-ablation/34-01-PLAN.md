<objective>
Add CLV (Closing Line Value) tracking to the prediction backtester. CLV measures whether the model's predicted spread was closer to the actual outcome than the Vegas closing line -- the gold standard for sports betting model evaluation.

Purpose: CLV provides a permanent model quality metric independent of ATS accuracy. Even if market features don't improve ATS, CLV tracking enables ongoing model monitoring.
Output: Three new functions in prediction_backtester.py, tests, and CLV reporting in the backtest CLI.
</objective>
