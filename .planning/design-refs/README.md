# Design References

## wc26-broadcast-scorebug.webp (captured 2026-07-11)

**This is what George means by "world cup themed."** Not just WC26 colors —
the FIFA26 BROADCAST SCOREBUG design language, from a CCTV5 frame of
Norway 0–2 France:

- **Container**: near-black navy bar, generous width, subtly rounded
  corners; floats over content like a broadcast overlay
- **Score treatment**: big bold condensed white digits flanking the GOLD
  TROPHY EMBLEM as the score separator (the signature move)
- **Identity**: flag chips at the outer edges; team names in bold
  condensed caps
- **Secondary info**: match clock in a small tab ABOVE the bar; scorer
  line ("DEMBÉLÉ 7', 20'") right-aligned inside the bar in small caps
- **Accents**: thin gold rules; a small ribbon/tab hanging BELOW the bar
  (fifa.com/tickets in the reference — CTA slot for us)
- **Two scales**: full-width bottom bug + compact corner bug (flags,
  abbreviated names, score, clock) — maps to hero component vs. inline
  list rows

NFL mappings: game predictions rows (predicted line where the clock
tab sits), matchup field-view header, games archive scores, live
scoreboards. Combine with the apple.com restraint (whitespace,
typography discipline) per the redesign seed — scorebug bars as the
identity moments inside an otherwise calm layout.
