      - From pbp_derived_df, extract `off_penalties` and `def_penalties` per team-week
      - Merge unpivoted_df (which has referee, game_id) with pbp_derived penalty columns on [team, season, week]

      - Map back to team rows: merge on [season, game_id, referee] to get ref_penalties_per_game per team-week

      - Fill Week 1 NaN from shift with defaults: wins=0, losses=0, ties=0

      - Games behind: within each (season, week, division): `leader_wins = groupby().transform("max")` on wins column. `games_behind_division_leader = leader_wins - wins`. (Not divided by 2 -- football uses straight win difference, not baseball half-game convention.)
      - Late season contention: `late_season_contention = (win_pct >= 0.400) & (week >= 10)`
      - Return DataFrame with: team, season, week, wins, losses, ties, win_pct, division_rank, games_behind_division_leader, late_season_contention
