# Sketch Manifest

## Design Direction
Apple.com layout grammar (one idea per full-bleed screen, huge restrained
typography, generous whitespace, scroll storytelling) fused with the FIFA
World Cup 26 broadcast scorebug identity (reference:
`.planning/design-refs/wc26-broadcast-scorebug.webp` — near-black navy
overlay bars, gold trophy emblem as score separator, bold condensed white
caps, team chips, clock tab above / ribbon below). Persistent floating AI
assistant styled as a mecha-inspired head (angular navy armor, gold V-fin,
glowing cyan eyes — inspired-by, never literal Gundam). The marketing home
leads with the provable claim: we beat the industry consensus, receipts
shown, misses included.

## Reference Points
- apple.com (layout grammar, typography restraint, translucent nav)
- FIFA26 broadcast scorebug (CCTV5 frame, saved in design-refs)
- Existing WC26 OKLCH tokens in web/frontend (`--wc-*`)
