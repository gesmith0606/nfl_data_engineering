# Sketch 001: Home Hero Direction

## Design Question
The redesign brief is apple.com layout grammar × FIFA26 broadcast scorebug
identity. This sketch tests where the dial sits between those two poles for
the marketing home page — the first thing an anonymous visitor sees.

## How to View
open .planning/sketches/001-home-hero-direction/index.html

## Variants
- **A: Apple Minimal** — white, huge restrained type, scorebug appears once
  as a jewel-like accent; closest to pure apple.com.
- **B: Broadcast Overlay** — the scorebug IS the hero, pitch-green field
  gradient, condensed caps everywhere, navy broadcast nav with gold rule;
  closest to the reference frame.
- **C: Dark Stadium Hybrid** — apple restraint at night: navy-black canvas,
  spotlight gradients, gold gradient headline, the receipt as a glassy
  scorebug-styled panel.

All three include: slim top nav (Home/Draft Room/Rankings/Scores/News/
Matchups/My League), the "we beat consensus" hero with REAL 2025 numbers
(QB −0.39 / WR −0.075 / TE −0.43, RB +0.26 shown honestly as WIP), a
predicted-game scorebug (gold trophy separator, clock tab = our line,
ribbon = CTA), a graph-intelligence teaser with real correlation chips
(Tua↔Hill +0.60 etc.), and the GX-01 mecha assistant button (bottom-right,
angular navy head, gold V-fin, glowing cyan eyes — click it, ask it
something, hit Enter).

## What to Look For
- Which direction makes the accuracy claim feel CREDIBLE (vs hype-y)?
- Does the scorebug read better as a rare jewel (A/C) or the main event (B)?
- Nav: apple-quiet (A/C) vs broadcast bar (B) — which fits the product?
- Dark (C) vs light (A): fantasy users check at night and on phones —
  use the 📱 toolbar button on each variant.
- Does the mecha assistant feel at home in all three? (It's identical
  across variants by design — judge it against each background.)
